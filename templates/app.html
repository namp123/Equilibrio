About

Around 450 million people currently suffer from such conditions, placing mental disorders among the leading causes of ill-health and disability worldwide. Out of which Anxiety is the most common mental disorder affecting nearly 40 million adults in the United States alone. Treatments ARE accessible, yet almost 66% of individuals with a realized mental problem never look for help from a health proffesional and the remainder of the populace are totally uninformed of their condition. Anxiety disorders are real, serious medical conditions - just as real and serious as physical disorders such as heart disease or diabetes and require the best medical attention.

Smartphones and mobile devices have become ubiquitous and with the rapid advancement of technology, the number of healthcare applications that are available for consumers on these devices are constantly growing. And hence we identified that the best solution to this unspoken worldwide issue is to create an application to help raise awareness, allow users to analyse the status of their mental health and seek appropriate professional aid if required. We hope to be helpful to our consumers in need, enabling them to identify the condition from the comforts of their own homes and consult a threapist or a counsellor just in the click of a button because we care and we only want the best for you.

At Equilibrio we have developed a smart and efficient way to keep track of your anxiety and maintain an overall mental balance and wellbeing. We know there is only so much we can do but remember there is always help, you are not alone. Speak to a family member or a proffesional guide immediately if you're experiencing an overwhelming amount of stress or anxiety.








Although our Predictor is extremely accurate and reliable, we advise you not to entirely depend on our system. Proffesional help is always better.
